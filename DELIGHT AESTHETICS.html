Delight Aesthetics FAQs

Permanent Makeup (PMU)

What is Permanent Makeup (PMU)?
Permanent Makeup, also known as cosmetic tattooing, involves using a specialized technique to deposit pigments into the skin to enhance features such as eyebrows, eyeliner, and lips.

How long does Permanent Makeup last?
PMU typically lasts between 1 to 3 years, depending on skin type, lifestyle, and aftercare. Touch-ups may be required to maintain the desired look.

Is the procedure painful?
A topical anesthetic is applied to minimize discomfort. Most clients report feeling only minor discomfort during the procedure.

What is the recovery time?
Initial healing takes about 7-10 days, with full healing taking up to 4 weeks. During this time, the color will settle, and any redness or swelling will subside.

Are there any side effects?
Common side effects include mild swelling, redness, and tenderness in the treated area. These usually subside within a few days.

How do I care for my Permanent Makeup?
Follow the aftercare instructions provided by your technician, which typically include keeping the area clean and moisturized, avoiding direct sunlight, and refraining from picking at the treated area.

Microneedling

What is Microneedling?
Microneedling is a cosmetic procedure that uses fine needles to create micro-injuries in the skin, stimulating collagen production and improving skin texture and tone.

What skin concerns can Microneedling address?
Microneedling can improve the appearance of acne scars, fine lines, wrinkles, enlarged pores, and overall skin texture.

Is Microneedling painful?
A topical numbing cream is applied to ensure comfort during the procedure. Clients typically report minimal discomfort.

How many sessions are needed?
The number of sessions varies based on individual skin concerns. Typically, a series of 3-6 sessions spaced 4-6 weeks apart is recommended for optimal results.

What is the downtime after Microneedling?
Most clients experience redness and mild swelling for 1-3 days post-treatment. Full recovery typically occurs within a week.

How should I care for my skin after Microneedling?
Avoid sun exposure, refrain from using harsh skincare products, and keep the skin moisturized. Follow the aftercare instructions provided by your technician.

Lash Extensions

What are Lash Extensions?
Lash extensions are semi-permanent fibers that are individually applied to your natural lashes to enhance length, fullness, and curl.

How long do Lash Extensions last?
Lash extensions typically last 4-6 weeks, with refills recommended every 2-3 weeks to maintain fullness.

Are Lash Extensions safe?
Yes, when applied by a trained professional, lash extensions are safe. Proper aftercare is essential to maintain the health of your natural lashes.

Can I wear makeup with Lash Extensions?
Yes, but avoid oil-based products as they can weaken the adhesive bond. Opt for water-based or lash extension-safe makeup.

How do I care for my Lash Extensions?
Avoid rubbing your eyes, keep them dry for the first 24 hours, and use a lash-safe cleanser to keep them clean.

Can Lash Extensions damage my natural lashes?
When applied correctly and with proper care, lash extensions should not damage your natural lashes.

Lash Lift

What is a Lash Lift?
A Lash Lift is a semi-permanent treatment that curls your natural lashes, giving them a lifted and lengthened appearance.

How long does a Lash Lift last?
A Lash Lift typically lasts 6-8 weeks, depending on your natural lash growth cycle.

Is a Lash Lift safe?
Yes, when performed by a trained professional, a Lash Lift is safe. The treatment uses gentle formulas to curl the lashes.

Can I wear mascara after a Lash Lift?
Yes, you can wear mascara after 24 hours post-treatment. However, many clients find they don’t need mascara due to the enhanced curl and length.

How do I care for my lashes after a Lash Lift?
Avoid water, steam, and rubbing your eyes for the first 24 hours. After that, you can treat them as you normally would.

Can I combine a Lash Lift with Lash Extensions?
It’s not recommended to combine a Lash Lift with Lash Extensions as the two treatments can compromise the integrity of your natural lashes.
